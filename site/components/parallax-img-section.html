Intelligence embedded, not bolted on

We approach AI and intelligent systems as architectural decisions, not add-on features.
That means designing for orchestration, automation, and decision-making at the system level, where models, agents, and workflows work together reliably in production.
We focus on trade-offs early: control vs. autonomy, performance vs. complexity, experimentation vs. operational stability. This allows intelligence to scale responsibly, integrate cleanly, and evolve alongside the systems it supports.
